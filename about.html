The Risks Our Solution

Our Mission is to Eliminate Technical Debt.

We believe that developers should spend their time building the future, not patching the past.

PyUpgrade was founded on a simple principle: the relentless march of technology should be an opportunity, not a burden. For years, we've seen brilliant engineering teams get bogged down by the weight of legacy systems. The cycle of patching, testing, and dealing with compatibility issues from outdated Python versions drains resources, stifles innovation, and demotivates talented developers.

We saw a clear need for an intelligent, automated solution that does more than just identify problems. We wanted to build a platform that accelerates the solution.

By harnessing the power of modern AI and building a deep understanding of the Python ecosystem, PyUpgrade provides a clear path forward. We automate the tedious work of assessment and provide actionable, AI-powered recommendations, allowing development teams to focus on what they do best: creating value and pushing the boundaries of what's possible.

Our goal is to make software modernization a seamless, continuous, and integrated part of the development lifecycle, ensuring that every codebase is secure, performant, and ready for the future.
